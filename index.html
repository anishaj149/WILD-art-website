Featured Organization:

Black Women's Health Imperative
All profits generated from the Big heArt donation drive will be going to the Black Women's Health Imperative (BWHI), a national organization dedicated to improving the mental and physical wellness of Black women in the US. We chose this organization because BWHI's unique mission resonates deeply with our core values of equity and inclusivity for women and nonbinary individuals. As an intersectional female empowerment organization, we stand in solidarity with the Black Lives Matter Movement and Black womxn around the world.
